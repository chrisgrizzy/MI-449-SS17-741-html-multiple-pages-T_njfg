Zapdos

  1. Zapdos is a dual-type Electric/Flying Legendary Pokémon introduced in Generation I
  2. It is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon
  3. it is one of the three Legendary birds of Kanto
  4. When Zapdos flaps its glittering wings, it releases electricity that can potentially cause thunderstorms
  5. When stricken by lightning, it gains power
  6. Zapdos reportedly appears only during thunderstorms, and is said to live among thunderclouds
