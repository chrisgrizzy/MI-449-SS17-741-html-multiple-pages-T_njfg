Articuno

  1. Articuno is an Ice/Flying-type Legendary Pokémon introduced in Generation I. It is part of the Kanto Legendary Birds, along with Zapdos and Moltres.
  2. It is one of the three Legendary birds of Kanto.
  3. It is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon.
  4. When Articuno flaps its wings, it can chill the moisture in the air around it, making snow fall. It flies from icy mountain to icy mountain.
  5. According to legend, it appears before doomed travelers lost in icy regions.
  6. Articuno’s name is a portmanteau created by combining “arctic” with “uno,” which is the Spanish word for “one.
  7. Articuno’s Japanese name is Freezer
