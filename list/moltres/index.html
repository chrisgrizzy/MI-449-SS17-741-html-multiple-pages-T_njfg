Moltres

  1. Moltres is a dual-type Fire/Flying Legendary Pokémon introduced in Generation I.
  2. It is not known to evolve into or from any other Pokémon.
  3. It is one of the three Legendary birds of Kanto.
  4. Moltres sheds embers with every flap of its wings, creating a brilliant flash of flames.
  5. By dipping itself into the magma of an active volcano, this Pokémon can heal itsel
  6. It migrates to the south with the coming of spring, and is said to bring an early springtime to cold lands.
